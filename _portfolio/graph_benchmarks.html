--- title: "Graph Benchmarks Table" excerpt: "Table of statistics and properties of commonly used GNN benchmark datasets." collection: portfolio --- This is my WIP reference for common GNN benchmark datasets. Notes:
  • All statistics are taken from the original papers or the PyG documentation. If you notice any discrepancies, missing important info etc. please drop me a(n) email/social media message!
  • For datasets with multiple graphs, the total number of nodes and edges across all graphs is reported.
  • No* in 'Directed?' field indicates that the dataset is typically undirected but the nature of the edges means that it could be converted to a directed graph.
